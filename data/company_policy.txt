Company Policies and Guidelines

1. Work Hours and Attendance
- Standard work hours: 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, Monday through Friday
- Flexible work arrangements available upon approval
- Remote work policy: Up to 3 days per week
- Overtime must be pre-approved by department manager

2. Leave Policy
Annual Leave:
- 20 days per calendar year
- Must be approved 2 weeks in advance
- Unused leave can be carried forward up to 10 days

Sick Leave:
- 10 days per calendar year
- Medical certificate required for 3+ consecutive days
- Emergency leave: Up to 5 days per year

3. Code of Conduct
Professional Behavior:
- Maintain professional communication
- Respect colleagues and clients
- No discrimination or harassment
- Maintain confidentiality
- Report unethical behavior

4. IT Security Policy
Password Requirements:
- Minimum 12 characters
- Must include uppercase, lowercase, numbers, and special characters
- Change every 90 days
- No password sharing

Data Protection:
- Encrypt sensitive data
- Use company VPN when working remotely
- Report security incidents immediately
- No unauthorized software installation

5. Communication Guidelines
Internal Communication:
- Use company email for work-related communication
- Respond to emails within 24 hours
- Use appropriate communication channels:
  * Email: Formal communication
  * Teams: Quick questions and team collaboration
  * Phone: Urgent matters

6. Performance Management
Review Cycles:
- Quarterly performance reviews
- Annual comprehensive review
- Continuous feedback encouraged

Goals and Objectives:
- Set SMART goals
- Regular progress tracking
- Performance improvement plans when needed

7. Health and Safety
Workplace Safety:
- Report hazards immediately
- Follow safety protocols
- Regular safety training
- Emergency procedures posted in common areas

8. Professional Development
Training:
- Annual training budget per employee
- Mandatory compliance training
- Skill development programs
- Conference attendance opportunities

9. Remote Work Guidelines
Requirements:
- Stable internet connection
- Secure workspace
- Regular check-ins
- Available during core hours
- Use company-approved tools

10. Social Media Policy
Guidelines:
- No confidential information sharing
- Professional conduct online
- Clear separation of personal and professional accounts
- Follow brand guidelines when representing company
